About CitizenAI

Revolutionizing Government-Citizen Interactions

CitizenAI is an intelligent citizen engagement platform designed to revolutionize how governments interact with the public. Leveraging Flask, IBM Granite models, and advanced AI technologies, CitizenAI provides real-time, AI-driven responses to citizen inquiries regarding government services, policies, and civic issues.
